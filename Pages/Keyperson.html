Key Persons

Our Key Persons are dedicated professionals who drive the mission of RSFPI forward. They manage daily operations, implement programs, and ensure that our initiatives effectively support underprivileged youth in Plaridel and nearby municipalities. Through their leadership and commitment, RSFPI continues to provide scholarships and educational assistance, empowering the next generation for a brighter future.
